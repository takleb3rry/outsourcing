## Scoring Formula

**Total = (Dim 1 × 0.50) + (Dim 2 × 0.30) + (Dim 3 × 0.20)**

- **Dim 1** (Credential Rigor & Domain Fit) = (Rigor + Domain Fit) ÷ 2 — *50% weight*
- **Dim 2** (Pitch Credibility) = Gate A (0/2/4) + Gate B (0/1/3) + Gate C (0/1/3) — *30% weight*
- **Dim 3** (Geographic Reach & Mid-Career Density) = (Geo Reach + Seniority) ÷ 2 — *20% weight*
